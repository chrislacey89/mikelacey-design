# Data Model

## Entities

### TimelineEvent
A milestone in Mike's 44-year career journey. Represents significant moments like starting a new job, moving to a new city, learning a new skill, or career breakthroughs. Used to build the visual timeline on the Story page.

### Credit
A production, show, or project Mike worked on. Includes the role he played (camera operator, technical director, director), the network or client, the year, and the category (sports or entertainment). Used in the Work section to showcase his portfolio.

### Testimonial
An "attaboy" or endorsement from a colleague or industry professional. Represents the written appreciation Mike has received over his career. Used in the Kudos section to build credibility through the words of others.

### Interview
A video interview featuring Mike, typically hosted on YouTube. Includes the video URL, title, and a brief description of the interview topic. Used in the Work section to let visitors hear directly from him.

### Photo
A career moment captured in an image. Includes the image file, a caption, and the year or context. Used throughout the site — in the gallery, timeline, and other sections — to visually tell Mike's story.

### ContactInfo
Mike's contact details used for the vCard QR code and contact page. Includes name, phone, email, website URL, and any other information that should pre-fill when someone scans the QR code.

## Relationships

All entities are standalone with no cross-references. This keeps the data simple to manage and allows each piece of content to be displayed independently across the site.

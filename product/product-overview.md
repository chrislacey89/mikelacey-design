# The Mike Lacey

## Description
A portfolio and career showcase for Mike Lacey, a television director with 44 years of experience in sports and entertainment production. The site tells his story from the ground up—literally starting in shipping at National Video Center—to directing PGA Tour Live, and invites future clients to book him for their next project.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Industry professionals need to see proof of experience
A visual career timeline featuring 44 years of credits, "attaboys" from colleagues, and video interviews that demonstrate depth and longevity in the business.

### Problem 2: Potential clients want to know he's the right fit
Showcases versatility across sports (PGA Tour, NASCAR, Olympic Judo) and entertainment (Nickelodeon, Disney, Ultra Music Fest, professional wrestling) with real work samples and credits.

### Problem 3: His story and philosophy matter
Personal narrative content highlighting his journey and approach: respect everyone, be passionate, make every show your best.

### Problem 4: Networking at events is hard to follow up on
Scannable QR code (vCard format) that instantly creates a contact on any phone—iOS or Android—with all his info pre-filled.

### Problem 5: Booking should be simple
Direct contact functionality to connect with him for future projects.

## Key Features
- Hero section with Golf Channel production image as background
- Featured headshot prominently displayed (placeholder until provided)
- Career timeline / visual journey (1981–present)
- Portfolio of work (sports & entertainment highlights)
- "Attaboys" / testimonials from industry colleagues
- Video interviews (YouTube embeds)
- Photo gallery showcasing career moments
- Bio / about section with his full story
- QR code contact card (vCard format, iOS & Android compatible)
- Contact / booking functionality

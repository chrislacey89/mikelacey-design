# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Home
The hero landing page featuring the Golf Channel production shot as background, Mike's headshot, and a compelling introduction that makes visitors want to learn more.

### 2. Story
The full career journey from 1981 to present, including the bio narrative, visual timeline, and his philosophy on the craft.

### 3. Work
Portfolio showcasing sports and entertainment credits, photo gallery of career moments, and embedded video interviews from YouTube.

### 4. Kudos
The "attaboys" and testimonials from industry colleagues, proving his reputation through the words of people he's worked with.

### 5. Connect
Contact form for booking inquiries, the QR code contact card (vCard), and any relevant social or professional links.

# Story Specification

## Overview
A scrolling narrative page that tells Mike's career journey from 1981 to present. Year markers appear alongside the text as visitors scroll, creating a sense of progression through the eras. The page ends with calls-to-action to explore his work or get in touch.

## User Flows
- Visitor scrolls through the narrative, seeing year markers as the story progresses through eras (NYC 1981, Orlando 1987, Freelance 1991, Directing breakthrough, Present day)
- Visitor reads Mike's philosophy naturally woven into the narrative
- Visitor reaches the end and clicks "See My Work" → navigates to Work section
- Visitor clicks "Get in Touch" → navigates to Connect section
- Visitor uses top navigation to jump to any section

## UI Requirements
- Scrolling narrative layout with year/era markers on the side
- Clean, readable typography for long-form content
- Optional photo slots alongside key eras (can be added later)
- Philosophy integrated naturally within narrative (no special callout treatment)
- Dual CTA buttons at the end: "See My Work" and "Get in Touch"
- Responsive layout for mobile (year markers may stack above content on small screens)
